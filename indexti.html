The musical taste that I listen to

In general, I listen to all kinds of songs depending on the meaning of the song, the singer, the beautiful rhythm and rhyme, and mostly on when the song is played. But rhyme and the beauty of the lyrics are more important to me. I mostly listen to old songs and old rap like generation one and two. I really enjoy listening to music while doing tasks, practicing, and at night.

Listening to beautiful protest rap by great rappers such as Yas and Hichkas, listening to beautiful old songs by artists like Vigen, Hayedeh, Mahasti, Moein, Setar, Dariush, Siavash Ghomayshi, and also foreign songs.
